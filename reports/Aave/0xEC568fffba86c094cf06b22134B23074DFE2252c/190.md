## Summary

A proposal to make parameter changes on Aave V3 ETH and V3 Arbitrum. For more details, please refer to this [forum post](https://governance.aave.com/t/arc-gauntlet-recommendations-for-aave-link-and-weth-on-v3-arbitrum-2023-03-21/12394/2?u=pauljlei) and Gauntlet's methodology for [borrow and supply caps](https://governance.aave.com/t/gauntlet-methodologies-borrow-and-supply-cap/11487).

## Specification

**V3 Arbitrum:**

- Increase WETH’s Supply Cap from 35,280 to 45,000

**V3 ETH:**

- Increase rETH’s Supply Cap from 10,000 to 20,000
- Decrease CRV's Supply Cap from 62,500,000 to 51,000,000

## Implementation

The proposal implements supply cap updates through two pre-deployed payloads, one for Ethereum and the other for Arbitrum:

- Ethereum Payload: [`0x142DCAEC322aAA25141B2597bf348487aDBd596d`](https://etherscan.io/address/0x142DCAEC322aAA25141B2597bf348487aDBd596d)
