## Summary

A proposal to make parameter changes on Aave V3 Optimism.

## Abstract

These parameter updates are a continuation of Gauntlet’s regular parameter recommendations.

## Motivation

Any market without supply caps could experience an outsized risk by someone opening a large position quickly. Maintaining supply caps that ensure that one user cannot subject the protocol to outsize risk is a key part of risk management for Aave v3 markets.

The supply cap can be revised lower if the community elects a more conservative approach. Regardless, the first step would be implementing an initial supply cap, which this proposal would accomplish.
